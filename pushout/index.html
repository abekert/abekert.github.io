Grab your friends and journey to magic islands with mysterious inhabitants. They cannot decide who is best: Mr. Crab or Vulcan, Mrs. Bird or Mr. Whale. Help them settle it with two, three or four players on one screen.

Mr. Lion dreams of becoming the fastest lion in the world. Explore every nook and cranny of the island and set a new world record.

You will also meet giraffe dummies and Crocodile the director. A cartoon island and live guitar soundtrack keep the competition bright at any age.
