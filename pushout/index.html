iHeartThisApp - App of the Week

Push Out is a hand-drawn arcade game for friends on one screen. Compete with up to four players on the same iPhone or iPad, push each other out of the arena, and settle the score between Mr. Crab, Vulcan, Mrs. Bird and Mr. Whale.

Or switch to single-player labyrinth mode and help Mr. Lion race through tropical island levels, unlock new challenges and set new world records.

Every character, background and animation frame was drawn by hand, then brought to life with parallax, SpriteKit animation and a live guitar soundtrack.

Game highlights

  • Local multiplayer for 2-4 players on one device
  • Single-player labyrinth mode
  • Game Center achievements and leaderboards
  • Hand-drawn characters and animation
  • Live guitar soundtrack
  • Localized into multiple languages

Archived indie iOS project

Push Out was originally released in 2014 as a small independent game for iPhone, iPad and iPod touch. This website is preserved as a project archive.

The App Store badge above points to the original release link. Availability may vary.

Built with Objective-C, SpriteKit and GameKit.
